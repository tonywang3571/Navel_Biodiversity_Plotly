Navel Biodiversity Dashboard

This webpage was created to interactively explore bacterial growth across different participant's navel. The horizonal bar graph displays the top 10 different bacteria, based on OTU (operational taxonomic unit) classification. The "gauge" chart displays the frequency of how many times each participant performs navel cleaning procedures (specific instructions for navel cleaning was not disclosed to us when the webpage was created and when the data was provided). The bubble chart displays different bacteria cultures per sample, as well as the value of each bacteria found with greater bubble radius for greater values of the bacteria.
